About us

Our team was gathered as the final year project team in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Moratuwa. We found the existing problem in our local domain in the document verification process. The IDStack project was started on February 2017. We hope that it has the potential to revolutionize the document verification protocol in Sri Lanka.
